About

Personal coaching without the guesswork.

I believe training should be practical, sustainable, and built around the individual. Your goals, experience, schedule, and equipment access all matter, so I do not hand every client the same generic plan.

We begin with a consultation, training history, goal review, and appropriate movement observations. From there, I build a clear program designed to help you get stronger, improve your exercise technique, and stay consistent.

I coach clients in Charlotte, NC in person and work with clients anywhere online through programmed workouts, form feedback, and weekly check-ins.

Frequently asked questions

What to know before getting started.

Do I need gym experience?

No. Coaching can be adjusted for complete beginners, including exercise setup, form, gym confidence, and basic training habits.

Where do in-person sessions happen?

Sessions are offered in the Charlotte, NC area. The exact location, access requirements, and any gym fees will be confirmed during your consultation.

What happens first?

We start with a consultation covering your goals, health and training history, current routine, and the coaching option that fits you best.

Do you provide nutrition support?

I can provide general nutrition education and habit-based guidance within a personal trainer’s scope. Medical nutrition therapy and individualized treatment should come from a qualified healthcare professional.

Can I train after an injury?

Training may be modified after you have appropriate medical clearance. Personal training does not replace diagnosis, physical therapy, or medical rehabilitation.

How quickly will you respond?

I typically respond to new consultation requests within 24 hours.
